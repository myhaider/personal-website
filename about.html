I am a student who is proud to be apart of the first class of undergrad Data Science majors here at UCSD. I enjoy dreaming up new ways of discovering the world through data, as well as being involved on campus as a student leader. One day I hope to make a big impact on this planet. Goals & Education

I am enrolled in Sixth College, where I am pursuing a B.S. in Data Science and a minor in Cognitive Science. Before that, I graduated from Laguna Hills High School with honors in 2017.

I am a member of the NASA Datanauts, a group that encourages collaboration amongst data scientists and advocacy for women in the field. Although I love being apart of this global community, I am also passionate about supporting right here in San Diego.

I served as Sixth College Senator at ASUCSD, the university’s main governing body. I acted as the liaison between Sixth College Student Council and AS Senate, and worked with my fellow senators to serve thousands of students throughout the term.

Currently, I am the Technology Director for Sixth College Technology Committee. I lead an amazing team that is in charge of planning events relating to gaming and tech, most notably Winter Gamefest, the largest student-run gaming convention on the west coast.

Interests

I’m an R and Tableau enthusiast who was born and raised in sunny soCal. I spend lots of time exploring San Diego, especially hiking trails and new foodie spots. I also love reading about the latest news in astronomy, computer animation, and soccer.
